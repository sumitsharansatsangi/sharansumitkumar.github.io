Introduction

Welcome to Kumpali's privacy policy page for our app, Mortgage. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

Our app, Mortgage, collects information from your Google Drive solely for the purpose of storing backup files. Access to your Drive is limited to specific files and does not include full access to your entire Drive.

We don't store any data on our servers. All data generated by the app is stored in Google Drive.

We don't even store your email , username or display name. We only store the file name and the date and time of the backup and that is on the google drive.

How We Use Information

The information collected is used to store backup files to ensure that you can recover your data when needed. This is an optional feature, and you can choose whether to enable it or not.

Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Drive API to store backup files. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data Storage and Security

Data accessed from Google Drive is processed in real-time. We don't store any data on our servers. All data is stored in Google Drive.

We use Google Drive API to store backup files. We do not share your data with any other third parties.

Data is always encrypted before being stored in Google Drive.
